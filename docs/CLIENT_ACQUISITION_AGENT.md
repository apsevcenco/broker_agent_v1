# Client Acquisition Agent

The Client Acquisition Agent is a planned growth and outreach module for Luxury Mobility AI OS.

## Purpose

Find and qualify potential clients across public online sources, social channels and communities, then prepare human-approved outreach and follow-up tasks.

## V1 Allowed Actions

- Research public prospect segments.
- Suggest target audiences.
- Draft outreach messages.
- Draft chat replies.
- Create follow-up tasks.
- Suggest lead score.
- Summarize prospect context.

## Requires Approval

- Any outbound message.
- Joining a group or community under a company account.
- Publishing ads or comments.
- Using contact data in a campaign.
- Connecting to an external platform.

## Blocked in V1

- Automatic sending.
- Automatic chat participation.
- Automatic ad posting.
- Scraping platforms.
- Bypassing rate limits.
- Impersonating people.
- Using bought contact lists.

## Future Integrations

- LinkedIn draft connector.
- Instagram draft connector.
- WhatsApp draft connector.
- Gmail draft connector.
- CRM enrichment.
- Campaign approval queue.
- Compliance/risk review before outreach.
