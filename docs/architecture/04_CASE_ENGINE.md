# Case Engine Blueprint

## Luxury Mobility AI OS

Version: 1.0 (Architecture Blueprint)

---

## 1. Purpose

The Case Engine is the operational heart of the AI Operating System.

Everything the company does is represented as a Case.

AI never works independently.

AI always works inside a Case.

A Case is the unit of business execution.

---

## 2. Vision

Traditional systems are built around:

- Emails
- CRM records
- Tasks
- Documents

Luxury Mobility AI OS is built around:

```text
Business Goal
↓
Case
↓
People + AI
↓
Execution
↓
Outcome
↓
Experience
```

Every business activity becomes a Case.

---

## 3. What Is A Case?

A Case represents a business objective from creation until completion.

Examples:

- Sell a yacht
- Buy a yacht
- Charter request
- Luxury car rental
- Marketing campaign
- Lead acquisition
- Customer support issue
- Translate legal documents
- Produce valuation report
- Research market
- Investor conversation

A Case is not:

- an email
- a task
- a lead
- a document

Those are events or resources belonging to a Case.

---

## 4. Case Lifecycle

Every Case follows the same universal lifecycle.

```text
Created
↓
Qualified
↓
Planned
↓
In Progress
↓
Waiting
↓
Review
↓
Completed
```

Alternative terminal states:

- Cancelled
- Archived

Profiles may extend this lifecycle but never replace it.

Example: Yacht Sale

```text
Created
↓
Qualification
↓
NDA
↓
POF
↓
Viewing
↓
Offer
↓
Negotiation
↓
Closing
↓
Completed
```

Example: Support Case

```text
Created
↓
Diagnosis
↓
Work
↓
Verification
↓
Completed
```

---

## 5. Case Object

Every Case contains:

- Identity
- Goal
- Participants
- Timeline
- Messages
- Documents
- Knowledge
- Memory
- Intelligence
- Tasks
- Tool Plans
- Agent Activity
- Operator Decisions
- Outcome
- Experience

This object becomes the Single Source of Truth.

---

## 6. Business Goal

Every Case begins with one Goal.

Examples:

- Sell M/Y Aurora
- Acquire 45m Explorer Yacht
- Generate 50 qualified leads
- Translate SPA
- Resolve client complaint
- Increase charter bookings

Without a Goal, a Case cannot exist.

---

## 7. Participants

Cases own participants.

Examples:

- Client
- Broker
- Operator
- Marketing
- Translator
- Support
- Lawyer
- Captain
- Owner
- Surveyor
- External Broker
- Family Office

Each participant has:

- Role
- Permissions
- Relationship
- Status
- History

---

## 8. AI Agents

AI Agents never own Cases.

They participate in Cases.

One Case may involve many agents.

Example:

```text
Lead Hunter
↓
Broker
↓
Translator
↓
Marketing
↓
Support
```

All collaborate on the same Case.

---

## 9. Messages

Messages are events.

A Case may contain:

- Email
- WhatsApp
- LinkedIn
- Instagram
- Telegram
- Phone call transcript
- Meeting notes
- System events

Messages never exist independently.

---

## 10. Intelligence

Every AI analysis belongs to the Case.

Includes:

- Perception
- Reasoning
- Decision
- Planning
- Execution Plan
- Learning
- Confidence
- Commercial Assessment
- History

Reasoning evolves over time.

---

## 11. Tool Plans

Tool Plans belong to the Case.

Tool Plans never execute automatically.

Every Tool Request follows this flow:

```text
Proposed
↓
Approved
↓
Executed
↓
Completed
```

Alternative terminal state:

- Rejected

---

## 12. Tasks

Tasks are generated from Tool Plans or Operators.

Tasks belong to the Case.

Not to the user.

---

## 13. Documents

Every document belongs to a Case.

Examples:

- NDA
- SPA
- MOA
- Valuation
- Survey
- Passport
- PDF
- Images
- Videos
- Emails
- Translations

---

## 14. Timeline

Everything becomes an event.

Example:

```text
Case Created
↓
Lead Identified
↓
Qualification Complete
↓
NDA Approved
↓
NDA Sent
↓
POF Received
↓
Viewing Scheduled
↓
Offer Received
↓
Negotiation
↓
Deal Closed
```

Nothing disappears.

Everything becomes history.

---

## 15. Decisions

Every important AI recommendation becomes a Decision.

Decision values:

- Proceed
- Proceed with Caution
- Need More Information
- Escalate
- Reject
- Archive

Operator decisions are stored forever.

---

## 16. Outcome

Every Case must end with an Outcome.

Outcome values:

- Won
- Lost
- Cancelled
- Completed
- Archived

Reason must always be captured.

---

## 17. Experience Engine

Closed Cases become company experience.

Examples:

- Why deal succeeded
- Why deal failed
- Successful outreach
- Failed outreach
- Negotiation lessons
- Buyer behaviour
- Market observations

Experience is company knowledge.

Not public knowledge.

---

## 18. Universal Design

Case Engine is business-independent.

It supports:

- Yacht Brokerage
- Charter
- Luxury Cars
- Marketing
- Lead Hunting
- Support
- Translation
- Research
- Future modules

No business-specific logic exists in the core.

Only Case Profiles extend behaviour.

---

## 19. Core Principles

- Everything starts with a Goal.
- Every Goal creates a Case.
- Every AI action belongs to a Case.
- Every Tool belongs to a Case.
- Every Document belongs to a Case.
- Every Message belongs to a Case.
- Every Decision belongs to a Case.
- Every Outcome belongs to a Case.
- Every Experience comes from a Case.

The Case is the operational center of the AI Operating System.

---

## 20. Future Extensions

Case Engine will later support:

- Sub-Cases
- Linked Cases
- Cross-company Cases
- Multi-agent collaboration
- Case Templates
- Automation Rules
- Workflow Designer
- Analytics
- Business KPIs
- Multi-tenant isolation
- Marketplace integrations

These extensions should not require changing the core architecture.
